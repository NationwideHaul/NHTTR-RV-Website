About Our Lakeland RV & Bus Repair Shop

More than a repair shop — we're a full trucking company that expanded into RV and bus service because we saw how badly the industry needed it.

A Full Trucking Company Behind Every Repair

Our Lakeland RV and bus service shop is part of Nationwide Haul — a full trucking company with sales, leasing, financing, insurance, and expert repair divisions. When we opened our doors for truck repair, RV and bus owners started showing up asking for help. They were tired of being turned away by shops that couldn't handle their vehicles, or worse, shops that took the job and got it wrong.

So we built a dedicated RV and bus division with the same standards that earned the trust of commercial fleets. The result is a repair facility backed by the infrastructure, diagnostic capability, and accountability of a full trucking operation — not a standalone shop hoping for the best.

Why We're Different

We don't just fix RVs and buses — we understand the full lifecycle of heavy vehicles. Our technicians came from the commercial truck world, where precision, diagnostics, and accountability are non-negotiable. That same discipline applies to every RV and bus that rolls into our shop.

We invested in 2026 diagnostic technology because guessing wastes your time and money. We became ASE Master Certified because your vehicle deserves more than a handyman with a socket set. And we built a team that treats your RV like a home and your bus like the lifeline it is — because that is exactly what they are.

What We Believe

Technical Empathy

We are authoritative on mechanics but protective of your living space. Your RV is your home, and we treat it that way — with clean hands, covered shoes, and genuine respect for the space you live in. Your bus carries precious cargo — passengers who depend on safe, reliable transportation. We never lose sight of the people behind the vehicle.

Honest Estimates, No Surprises

We tell you what we find, what it costs, and what we recommend — before we start work. No upselling, no hidden fees, no pressure to approve repairs you don't need. If a repair isn't necessary, we will tell you. If there is a more affordable solution that gets you safely back on the road, we will recommend it. Your trust is worth more to us than any single invoice.

Total RV Care

Most shops work on either the engine OR the living space. We handle both under one roof. One trusted team for your entire vehicle — from the chassis to the kitchen sink. That means fewer appointments, faster turnarounds, and a single point of accountability for everything on your RV or bus. No more bouncing between a diesel mechanic and an RV interior specialist.

Warranty-Friendly

We work with extended warranties and RV protection plans including Good Sam, Wholesale Warranties, and coach manufacturer programs. We handle the paperwork, coordinate with the warranty company, and fight for the coverage you paid for — so you can focus on the road ahead instead of claim forms and hold music.

What Makes Nationwide Haul Different

  • Full Trucking Company — sales, leasing, financing, insurance, and expert repair under one roof. Our RV and bus division is backed by the infrastructure of an entire commercial operation.
  • ASE Master Certified Technicians with 15+ years of combined experience across diesel engines, chassis systems, and RV living space components
  • 2026 Diagnostic Technology reads every onboard module — engine, transmission, ABS, multiplex, leveling, and generator systems
  • Total RV Care — chassis AND living space, one shop, one trusted team, one point of contact for your entire vehicle
  • Warranty-Friendly Service — we work with all major RV protection plans and handle the claims process for you
  • Central Florida Location — minutes from I-4, easy access from Tampa, Orlando, Winter Haven, and all of Central Florida

Our Divisions

Truck & Trailer Sales

New and used commercial trucks and trailers for owner-operators, small fleets, and growing businesses. Our sales team understands the equipment because we service it every day. That means honest recommendations and vehicles that are ready to work from day one.

Leasing & Financing

Flexible leasing and financing programs designed to keep operations growing. Whether you are an owner-operator looking for your first truck or a fleet manager expanding capacity, we offer programs that fit real-world budgets and timelines.

Insurance Services

Commercial vehicle insurance for operators and fleets. We understand the coverage requirements for heavy vehicles because we work on them daily. Our insurance division helps you find the right protection without overpaying for coverage you don't need.

Service & Repair

Our Lakeland shop handles everything from routine RV maintenance to major bus overhauls, DOT inspections, and roadside assistance. This is where the rubber meets the road — the division that earned our reputation and the one that RV owners and bus fleet managers across Central Florida trust with their vehicles. From a simple oil change to a complete diesel engine overhaul, our technicians bring the same precision and accountability to every job.
