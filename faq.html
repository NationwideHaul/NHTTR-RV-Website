Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our RV and bus repair services, our shop, and how we work.

General Questions

What types of vehicles do you service?

We specialize in RVs (Class A Diesel Pushers, Class C & Gas Motorhomes, Fifth Wheels, Travel Trailers) and buses (school buses, commercial coaches, transit buses, shuttle and church buses). We service all major brands including Tiffin, Newmar, Winnebago, Thor, Fleetwood, Blue Bird, Thomas Built, IC Bus, Prevost, and more.

What are your business hours?

Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Closed Saturday and Sunday. RV roadside assistance available during business hours.

Where are you located?

5021 Frontage Road N., Suite RV, Lakeland, FL 33810—just minutes from I-4, US-98, and Polk Parkway. Easy access from Tampa, Orlando, and all of Central Florida.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended to minimize wait times, but we accept walk-ins when possible. Schedule online or call (863) 434-8893.

RV Services & Repairs

Do you work on both the engine AND the living space?

Yes. That is our Total RV Care approach. Most shops only handle one side—we service the chassis, drivetrain, engine, AND the living space (roof, slide-outs, A/C, plumbing, appliances, electrical) under one roof. One trusted team for your entire RV.

Do you service diesel pushers?

Absolutely. We specialize in Class A Diesel Pushers with Cummins, Caterpillar, and other diesel platforms. Our 2026 diagnostic technology and ASE Master Certified technicians handle everything from engine service to slide-out repair.

Can you fix my RV roof leak?

Yes. Roof leaks are one of our most common repairs. We inspect every seam, vent, skylight, and A/C mount to find the source. We use the correct sealant for your roof type (EPDM, TPO, fiberglass) and can do everything from spot repairs to full membrane replacement.

Do you work on RV generators?

Yes. We service Cummins Onan, Generac, Kohler, and other RV generator brands. From routine maintenance (oil changes, filters, spark plugs) to major repairs (control boards, stators, fuel systems). We stock common Onan parts for faster turnaround. Learn more about our generator service.

Do you offer RV roadside assistance?

Yes, during business hours. If your RV breaks down on I-4, US-98, or anywhere in Central Florida, call (863) 434-8893. Our mobile service truck carries diagnostic tools and common parts. Learn more about roadside assistance.

Bus & Fleet Services

Do you service school buses?

Yes. We service Blue Bird, Thomas Built, IC Bus, and other school bus brands. Engine, brakes, A/C, DOT inspections, safety systems, wheelchair lifts—everything to keep your fleet safe and compliant. Learn more about school bus repair.

Do you perform DOT inspections?

Yes. We perform FMCSA-compliant annual DOT inspections for commercial vehicles. We also offer voluntary safety inspections for private RV owners before long trips. Learn more about DOT & safety inspections.

Do you offer fleet maintenance programs?

Yes. We offer customized PM programs for bus fleets including priority scheduling, centralized billing, maintenance tracking, and DOT compliance documentation. Learn more about preventative maintenance.

Warranties & Insurance

Do you work with RV warranties and protection plans?

Yes. We are warranty-friendly and work with most extended warranties and RV protection plans. We handle the claims paperwork so you can focus on getting back on the road.

Do you provide estimates before starting work?

Absolutely. We provide transparent, detailed estimates before beginning any repair. No surprises—you will know exactly what needs to be done and what it costs before we touch your vehicle.

Billing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, checks, all major credit cards, and fleet/commercial accounts. For fleet customers, we offer centralized billing and net terms.
