Contact Information

Phone

Call us directly at (863) 774-4570. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Whether you have a question about your RV, need a repair estimate, or want to schedule service, we are happy to help over the phone. Email

Send us an email at lakelandservice@nationwidehaul.com. We typically respond within one business day. Email is a great option if you want to describe your issue in detail, attach photos of the problem, or request a preliminary estimate before bringing your RV or bus to the shop.

RV Roadside Assistance

Available during business hours, Monday through Friday. If you are broken down on I-4, US-98, or anywhere in Central Florida, call (863) 774-4570 and let us know your location. Our mobile service technicians will arrive equipped to diagnose the issue on-site and either perform a roadside repair or arrange a safe tow back to our Lakeland facility. We understand that a disabled RV on a highway shoulder is stressful—especially with family on board—and we prioritize getting to you quickly.

Include as much detail as you can about the problem so our technicians can prepare the right tools, parts, and diagnostic equipment before you arrive.
